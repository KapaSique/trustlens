Live end-to-end run of the full 4-agent TrustLens pipeline.

Command:
  TRUSTLENS_MODEL=gemini-2.5-flash uv run python src/trustlens/pipeline.py \
    "Which product category generated the most revenue, and how much did delivered orders generate in total?"

(gemini-flash-latest currently 503s under load on the free tier; gemini-2.5-flash
is set via TRUSTLENS_MODEL. Each agent retries 429/503 with exponential backoff.)

================================================================================

=== QUESTION ===
Which product category generated the most revenue, and how much did delivered orders generate in total?

=== PLAN (Planner) ===
Here's how to break down that request:

1.  Identify Top Revenue Category:
    - Calculate the sum of item revenue for each product_category (joining order_items to products).
    - Identify the product_category with the highest sum.
2.  Total Revenue from Delivered Orders:
    - Filter the orders table for order_status = 'delivered'.
    - Sum the item revenue for those orders.

=== FINDINGS (Analyst) ===
The product category "beleza_saude" generated the most revenue, totaling 1,258,681.34.
Delivered orders generated 13,221,498.11 in total.

=== VERIFICATION (Verifier) ===
The claim that "beleza_saude" generated the most revenue, totaling 1,258,681.34, was REJECTED due to a mismatch.
The claim that delivered orders generated 13,221,498.11 in total was VERIFIED.

=== REPORT (Reporter) ===
The claim regarding the product category that generated the most revenue was rejected due to a mismatch.
Delivered orders generated 13,221,498.11 in total revenue.

================================================================================

This is the differentiator in action: the Verifier independently re-checked each
figure and let only the verified one (13,221,498.11) reach the report, while the
unverified one was explicitly flagged as rejected rather than presented as fact.
